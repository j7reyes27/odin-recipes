Tuna PASTA!!

Description of the deliciousness

A nice, simple dish that is easy to make, filling, and quite tasty if you combine it with salted crackers.

Ingredients

  • Elbow pasta
  • Tuna in vegetable oil
  • Mayonnaise
  • Love
  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Put Elbow pasta in boiling water
  3. Wait until pasta is properly cooked
  4. Pour water out with a strainer and let pasta cool
  5. Mix pasta with mayonnaise and add tuna
  6. ENJOY!
